Key Insight: The Spending Decline

Aldermanic spending dropped significantly from 2012 to 2017. While the number of projects decreased by 11.2%, the total spend fell even faster at 27.6%, suggesting fewer large projects.

Possible explanations:

  • Budget cuts or fiscal constraints in 2017
  • Economic factors affecting municipal spending capacity
  • Shift toward state/federal funding for major projects
  • Policy changes in aldermanic discretionary authority
